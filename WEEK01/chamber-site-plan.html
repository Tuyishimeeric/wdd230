Chamber Site Plan

Site Name

The site name will be Kigali Chamber of Commerce. The name was chosen because Springfield is a well-known city with a vibrant business community, and it reflects the local focus of the chamber. The name "Chamber" is included to clarify the site's purpose and audience as a regional business resource.

Site Purpose

The Kigali Chamber of Commerce website aims to:

  • Provide resources and support for local businesses to grow and thrive through networking, events, and services.
  • Act as a hub for economic and community development by keeping the public informed about key issues affecting the area.

Scenarios

Here are some questions that site visitors might ask:

  • What upcoming events does the chamber offer for networking and business development?
  • How can I become a member of the Springfield Chamber of Commerce?
  • What is the current state of local economic growth and development?

Color Schema

The selected color palette will help create a professional, welcoming feel for the site.

  • Primary Color: #005f73 (Dark Teal) - Used for headers, navigation, and calls to action.
  • Secondary Color: #94d2bd (Light Teal) - Used for background elements, borders, and secondary buttons.

Typography

The typography selections will help create a clean, modern look for the site:

  • Primary Font: 'Roboto', sans-serif - Used for body text and paragraphs for readability.
  • Secondary Font: 'Merriweather', serif - Used for headings to add contrast and importance.
